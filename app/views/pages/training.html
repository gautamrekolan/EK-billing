Alida Burkholder has a simple training philosophy - to work long and hard in order to get the best out of her clients and their horses. She keeps a very manageable number of horses in training (generally 5-7), which results in a lot of individualized focus and attention. Alida specializes in a wide variety of disciplines, from Youth/Non-Pro all around to halter horses. Her young horses are started the right way, with longevity and long-term physical and mental soundness as a primary goal.
